Enviromental standards
An environmentally responsible workplace
We take environmental standards and sustainability seriously. We are proud to announce that e.town 6 have been awarded LEED Platinum certification, the highest rating possible by the U.S. Green Building Council. This certification demonstrates our commitment to reducing the building's carbon footprint and promoting a healthier work environment for our tenants.
LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) certification is a globally recognized rating system for green buildings. To achieve LEED Platinum certification, a building must meet rigorous standards in areas such as energy efficiency, water conservation, indoor air quality, and materials selection. Our building has implemented various sustainable features, such as a green roof to reduce heat absorption, energy-efficient lighting and HVAC systems, and a rainwater harvesting system to conserve water.
By choosing to lease an office space in e.town 6, you can demonstrate your company's commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility. This not only benefits the environment, but also has the potential to increase employee productivity and well-being, as well as attract environmentally conscious customers and clients.
In e.town 6, we are proud to set an example for sustainable commercial real estate and are dedicated to providing our tenants with a healthy and environmentally responsible workplace.
